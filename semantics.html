10 Semantic Elements Used

Header

Defines the introductory content of the page.

Main

Represents the primary content of the document.

Section

Groups related content into meaningful sections.

Article

Holds self-contained content such as a news post.

Aside

Contains supporting or related information.

Figure

Wraps media content like images.

Figcaption

Provides a caption for a figure.

Footer

Contains closing information and links.

Time

Represents a specific time or date.

Details

Hides extra information until opened.
